Tech Avinya

Tech-Avinya 2024 is NIT Nagaland's first-ever tech fest, designed to be a landmark event celebrating technology, innovation, and creativity. This festival offers a dynamic platform for students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts to come together and participate in a variety of events, including coding challenges, hackathons, robotics, AI, and more. Tech-Avinya encourages collaboration, creativity, and problem-solving, providing opportunities to showcase skills and gain hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies. The fest will feature workshops, competitions, and seminars, focusing on emerging fields like machine learning, blockchain, and IoT, giving participants a chance to enhance their knowledge and engage with industry experts. Tech-Avinya is more than just a technical event – it's a chance to learn, network, and be inspired by the latest advancements in technology. Get ready to be part of this exciting, enriching experience!

About

Ekarikthin joins hands with Tech-Avinya to create a unique fusion of culture and technology at NIT Nagaland. Blending vibrant traditions with cutting-edge tech events, the fest offers thrilling competitions, workshops, and performances, celebrating creativity, innovation, and heritage in one dynamic platform. It's an unmissable opportunity to learn, collaborate, and be inspired!
