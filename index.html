About

Ekarikthin

Ekarikthin is the annual Techno-Cultural Festival of NIT Nagaland, blending culture and technology through a variety of competitions, showcases, and exhibitions. The event brings together students, industry leaders, and creatives, fostering collaboration and innovation. It celebrates passion, creativity, and community spirit, offering a platform for talent to shine. Ekarikthin is more than just a festival—it's a vibrant celebration of ideas, diversity, and boundless potential.

Tech Avinya

Tech-Avinya - NIT Nagaland's first-ever tech fest, is here to ignite innovation and creativity! A platform for tech enthusiasts to showcase skills, compete in exciting challenges, and explore the latest advancements, Tech-Avinya promises an unforgettable journey of learning and collaboration. Join us for the ultimate celebration of technology and ingenuity!
